Know To Win

Welcome to Know To Win

  • A game to test your smarts with fun quizzes!
  • Play in tons of topics and win prizes.
  • Made for everyone, young to old.
  • Join and see how much you know!

Hello, new player! “Know To Win” is a cool game where you answer questions to win awesome stuff. Our quizzes cover heaps of topics—like Biology, Coding, or even Space—created by clever AI. It’s perfect for kids, teens, or adults, anyone who loves a challenge! You just sign up and start playing to test your brain. It’s super fun and easy to dive in, no matter your age.

Where You Can Play

  • Find it on Play Store for Android.
  • Get it on Apple Store for iPhone.
  • Play in any browser—like Chrome.
  • Pick your device and start!

You can enjoy “Know To Win” on your favorite device! If you’ve got an Android phone, download it from the Play Store. iPhone fans can grab it from the Apple Store. Or, if you’re at a computer, just open a browser—like Chrome or Firefox—and play right there. No special gear needed—just choose how you want to play and jump in. It’s that simple for newbies like you!

How the Game Works

  • Sign up and play AI-made quizzes.
  • Pay credits to join each game.
  • Answer right to win more credits.
  • Pick from tons of topics!

Here’s how it starts: you join “Know To Win” and pick a quiz to play. These aren’t boring—they’re made by AI and cover cool stuff like Astronomy or Robotics! You use credits (like game coins) to enter each quiz. Answer the questions right, and you’ll win credits back—sometimes even more than you spent. It’s a blast for new players, and there’s so much to explore with all the topics available.

All About Credits

  • Four kinds: Earned, Donated, Referral, Purchased.
  • Any type works to enter quizzes.
  • Wins go to Earned Credits only.
  • Earned Credits get you prizes.

Credits are your key to playing! There are four types: Earned (you win them), Donated (friends give them), Referral (invite pals to get some), and Purchased (buy them). You can use any of these to join a quiz—no limits there. But when you win, those credits stack up in your Earned Credits pile. That’s the special one, because only Earned Credits let you grab prizes later. Simple and exciting for a beginner!

Winning Prizes

  • Prizes: gift cards or real items.
  • Add to cart whenever you want.
  • Checkout only during weekly windows.
  • Need Earned Credits and top rank.

With your Earned Credits, you can pick prizes—like digital gift cards or real things you can hold! Browse anytime and toss what you like into your cart. But to actually get them, you wait for a weekly checkout window—like a prize day. To check out, you need enough Earned Credits and a spot in the top 100 players for improvement. It’s a fun goal for new players to aim for!

Levels and Topics

  • Quizzes have Category, Subject, Level.
  • Levels: 1-17 (school to expert).
  • Topics like Maths, Physics, AI.
  • Improve by climbing levels weekly.

Every quiz has three parts: a Category (like Physics), a Subject (like Optics), and a Level (1 to 17—1-12 for school, 13-16 for college, 17 for pros). There are tons of options—think Coding with Python, Biology with Genetics, or even Space with Exoplanets! You start at a level and try to move up each week. As a newbie, it’s exciting to see how far you can go in your favorite topics.

Becoming a Winner

  • Improve levels to hit top 100.
  • Start level must be 2+ to count.
  • Example: 5 triplets, total +6 levels.
  • Top rank + credits = prizes!

To win, you improve your levels weekly—like Physics-Optics from 2 to 4 (+2). Your starting level must be 2 or higher (if none in 30 days, it’s 0 or 1, which doesn’t count). Below’s an example: start with 5 triplets, end higher, total +6. The top 100 improvers get on the leaderboard. With enough Earned Credits, you checkout prizes. New? Just play and grow!

Start (1 Week Ago): Physics-Optics-2, Maths-Calculus-3, AI-Chatbots-4, Biology-Genetics-2, Coding-Python-5

End (Now): Physics-Optics-4 (+2), Maths-Calculus-4 (+1), AI-Chatbots-5 (+1), Biology-Genetics-3 (+1), Coding-Python-6 (+1) = +6 total
