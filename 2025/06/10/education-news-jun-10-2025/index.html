Top 10 Education News in India (June 8-10, 2025)

1. IIT Delhi Launches BTech in Design for 2025 Admissions
IIT Delhi is introducing a four-year BTech in Design program starting from the 2025-26 academic year, with an intake of 20 students. Admission requires JEE Advanced and UCEED scores, blending engineering with creative problem-solving.
Source: Indian Express
2. Maharashtra FYJC Admission 2025: Final Merit List on June 11
The final merit list for Maharashtra FYJC (Class 11) admissions will be released on June 11, 2025, following a revised schedule with grievance submissions on June 9.
Source: Shiksha
3. OpenAI’s ChatGPT o3 Achieves Near-Perfect Score in JEE Advanced 2025
OpenAI’s ChatGPT o3 scored 327/360 in the JEE Advanced 2025, securing an All India Rank of 4, raising discussions about AI’s role in competitive exams.
Source: Financial Express
4. CUET Reshaping College Admissions in India
An opinion piece highlights how CUET is making college admissions fairer and more accessible, supporting India’s goal of a 50% Gross Enrollment Rate.

Source: Indian Express
